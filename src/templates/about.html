About Our XSpare

In the present time where the main focus of the world is to renovate and reuse things, we still lag in the field of electronics and equipment reusability. As soon as something goes wrong we dispose of the things. The main objective of this website is about initiating the platform for exchange of spare parts between those who have and those who need them.

Spare parts- Refers to all the functional parts of different equipment.

A platform for exchange of spare parts between those who have and those who need them - for cars, home equipment, computers, bikes and other electronics etc., by implementing direct association of offer (people offering spare pieces) and demand (people looking for spare pieces).
